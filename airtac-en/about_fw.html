About Load factor (fw)

The load of linear guide can be calculated.

But equipments tend to involve vibrations or impacts during operation, which means actual load is generally greater than the calculated value.

Hence, the basic load rating(C) shall be divided by load factor in the following table when affected by vibrations or impacts.

About Load factor (fw)
Loading condition Service velocity fw
No impacts or vibrations V≤0.25m/s 1.0～1.2
Normal impacts or vibrations 0.25＜V≤1.0m/s 1.2～1.5
Medium impacts or vibrations 1.0＜V≤2.0m/s 1.5～2.0
Strong impacts or vibrations V＞2.0m/s 2.0～3.5
